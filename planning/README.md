• Project Description : 
For university student who need to keep fit and stay healthy, our Healthy Lifestyle App is a digital wellness and campus health platform that provides personalized health monitoring and access to the campus medical support. It includes features such as activity tracker, hydration tracking and mental wellness mood tracking. Unlike other platforms in the market, our product offers health and wellbeing support that is tailored to the academic calendar and student lifestyle.
